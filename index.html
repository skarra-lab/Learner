Life Lessons

You Can't Always Get What You Want.
Sometimes you Hope to achieve certain things
but life turns out the least you expected.
Now te best way to avoid depression and anxiety from
this is accepting within yourself that we don't plan
things we hope that they happen according to our
desires but there is one person who decide if things
do really happen, God.
Things happen in God's way, you just need as a
person to have more than 1 or 2 ideas/plans.
Yes it is possible for both your plans to fail
but the third one would only fail if you don't put
enough effort.

Life Lessons

Pace Yourself.
Competition is another slow poison that people must
try to avoid. We often compete with people who are even too busy
to be competing against us.
As a person set your own pace(not influenced by someones
progress) so you can make sure you execute all of your
plan very well with no rush. Everything that's
meant for you will always come to you rushing
things isn't gonna help you with anything.
